## Layout Rules

- Use page heroes for major entry points, not every internal view.
- Keep section rhythm consistent so component examples feel like one guide.
- Use grids and columns to compare related items, not to force unrelated guidance into one surface.
- Keep logo rows, anchors, and page links secondary to the page title and primary task.
- Put repeated shell behavior in the layer instead of rebuilding header, footer, and container spacing per page.
