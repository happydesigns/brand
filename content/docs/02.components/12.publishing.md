## Publishing Rules

- Use blog and changelog components when entries need chronology, authorship, release context, or comparison.
- Keep pricing surfaces practical. Emphasize choices, constraints, and next actions over decoration.
- Use marquee motion only for supporting proof or compact partner/token rows.
- Avoid turning editorial pages into dashboards; let the content carry most of the expression.
- Keep CTAs grounded in the reader's next decision.
