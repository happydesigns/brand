A useful brand guide documents how the brand sounds in headings, labels, errors, and supporting copy, not only how it looks.

## Attributes

- Thoughtful
- Warm
- Precise
- Maintainable

## Do

- Use concrete nouns and direct verbs.
- Explain decisions through durable rules.
- Keep interface labels short and action-oriented.

## Do Not

- Do not over-explain simple controls.
- Do not rely on hype, vague claims, or decorative wording.
- Do not let brand copy obscure product behavior.

## Interface Copy

Good interface copy should help users act. It should not advertise the interface back to them.
