# happydesigns logo assets

## Primary everyday identity

- `happydesigns-wordmark.svg` — typographic happydesigns. logo for light surfaces.
- `happydesigns-wordmark-inverse.svg` — typographic happydesigns. logo for dark surfaces.

## Primary standalone symbol

- `happydesigns-symbol.svg` — full colorful symbol with h-shape, soft peach field, coral dot, and white rounded tile. Unchanged across light and dark mode.

## Utility / production symbols

- `happydesigns-symbol-reduced.svg` — simplified h + coral dot mark for light surfaces.
- `happydesigns-symbol-reduced-inverse.svg` — simplified h + coral dot mark for dark surfaces.
- `happydesigns-symbol-reduced-tile.svg` — reduced symbol on a light tile.
- `happydesigns-symbol-reduced-tile-inverse.svg` — reduced symbol on a dark tile.

## Teaching / introduction lockups

- `happydesigns-lockup.svg` — full symbol + wordmark for light surfaces.
- `happydesigns-lockup-inverse.svg` — full symbol + wordmark for dark surfaces.

## Constrained / production lockups

- `happydesigns-lockup-reduced.svg` — reduced symbol + wordmark for light surfaces.
- `happydesigns-lockup-reduced-inverse.svg` — reduced symbol + wordmark for dark surfaces.

## Brand-owned compositions

- `happydesigns-signature.svg` — standardized brand composition for footers and brand sections, light surfaces.
- `happydesigns-signature-inverse.svg` — brand signature for dark surfaces.

## Other

- `happydesigns-favicon.png` — PNG fallback for app icon and touch icon contexts.

## Canonical logo colors

- Graphite: `#242423`
- Warm White: `#FAF7F2`
- Soft Peach: `#FFE4D4`
- Coral: `#F28564`

## Notes

The full symbol (`happydesigns-symbol.svg`) behaves like a branded object — it does not change between light and dark mode.

Dark-surface variants use the suffix `-inverse`. The suffix `-dark` is not used.

The reduced symbol and reduced lockup are utility/production variants, not primary brand marks.

The brand signature is a standardized brand application, not the canonical logo.
